About Yoga


 

Yoga is a practice that integrates physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama) and meditation (raja), with an aim to unite the body, mind & spirit, promoting well-being & balance.

Benefits of Yoga


 
  • Enhances both flexibility and muscular strength.
  • Improves focus and sharpens the mind
  • Helps alleviate stress and calm anxiety
  • Encourages deep relaxation and supports restful sleep
  • Strengthens the body's natural defense system

Types of Yoga


There are basic and simple yoga poses that beginners can do.

  • Ashtanga - involves synchronizing breath with a series of postures
  • Hatha - focuses on physical postures and breath control
  • Kundalini - sequence of physical postures, chanting, and special breathing techniques
  • Raja - a practice focused on controlling the mind and achieving mental mastery through meditations
  • Vinyasa - a creative form of yoga where poses are linked together with the breath in a flowing sequence (dancing)
